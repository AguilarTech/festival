Downfield Festival

22 - 24 August 2024 | Hampshire, UK

Welcome to Downfield

Nestled in the heart of the Hampshire countryside, Downfield is an intimate weekend festival that brings together a curated lineup of groundbreaking musicians, visionary artists, and a community of passionate festival-goers. Born from a desire to create a more personal and immersive festival experience, we're all about discovery, connection, and creating memories that last a lifetime.

Expect three days of incredible sounds across two stages, from indie rock and electronic beats to soulful folk and jazz. Explore stunning art installations, participate in workshops, and enjoy delicious food from local vendors. At Downfield, we're building more than a festival; we're building a family.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the festival dates?

The festival runs from Friday, 22nd August to Sunday, 24th August 2024.

Can I camp at the festival?

Yes, your weekend ticket includes access to our general campsite. We also have options for boutique camping and campervan passes available for purchase.

Is the festival family-friendly?

Absolutely! We welcome festival-goers of all ages. Children under 12 can attend for free. Please note anyone under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.

How do I get there?

We're located near Winchester, Hampshire. Full travel details, including information on shuttle buses from the nearest train station, will be sent to ticket holders closer to the event.
